Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take requests? +

Yes! I'm always happy to take requests and work with you to create the perfect playlist for your event. Just let me know your preferences in advance.

What music genres do you play? +

Breakbeat and indobounce are my specialties - these high-energy genres are what I do best. But I'm versatile and can mix other genres to match your event's vibe. Whether you want hip-hop, house, EDM, or Top 40 hits, I'll read the crowd and keep the energy flowing!

How far in advance should I book? +

I recommend booking at least 1 month in advance to secure your date, especially for weekend events. However, reach out anytime - I may have last-minute availability!

Do you provide equipment? +

I provide my professional DJ controller (PIONEER DDJ-REV1), laptop (ACER NITRO V 15), and chargers. However, I do NOT provide speaker systems or RCA cables - the venue must have their own sound system that I can connect to.

What's your cancellation policy? +

A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. Please note that deposits are non-refundable in case of cancellation. This ensures I keep your date reserved exclusively for you.

Do you offer MC services? +

Absolutely! I can serve as your MC/hype man during the event, making announcements, engaging with the crowd, and keeping the energy high throughout the night.

Can you play at outdoor venues? +

Yes, I can perform at outdoor venues! I've done outdoor events 3-4 times before. However, if there's rain or any situation involving water/moisture, I won't be able to perform as equipment safety is my top priority.

Do you charge for transportation? +

For events within Bekasi area, transportation is included in the package price. However, for events outside Bekasi (such as Jakarta, Depok, Tangerang, etc.), a separate transportation fee applies to cover fuel and travel costs. This will be discussed during booking confirmation.
